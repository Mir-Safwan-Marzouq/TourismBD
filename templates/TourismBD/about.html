About US

Bangladesh is a very beautiful country. Because of less promotion we are not being able to catch the market of foreign tourists and also our own travellers sometimes struggle to find perfect guide. In my CSE 3100 project an idea came to my mind to represent the whole country in a way that will attract foreign tourists to tour our country and also help our travellers and also attract new people to start travelling.
